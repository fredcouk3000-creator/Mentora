Whether you're a parent or a tutor, here's exactly what happens, step by step.

Step by Step

Your child's tutoring journey

1

Create your free account

Register as a parent and set up learner profiles for each of your children. Tell us their level (Primary, JSS, or SSS), the subjects they need help with, and whether you prefer in-person or virtual sessions.

🎉 Account creation is completely free. No subscription required.
2

Search and compare tutors

Browse our verified tutor profiles. Filter by subject, school level (Primary, JSS, SSS, or JAMB Prep), location, delivery mode, price, and rating. Read detailed profiles including qualifications, experience, and genuine parent reviews.

3

Book and pay safely

Select your preferred tutor, pick a date and time, and pay securely. Your money is held safely and is only released to the tutor after the session is confirmed complete.

🔒 Your payment is fully protected from the moment you book.
4

Attend the session

For virtual sessions, join through our integrated video room. No extra software needed. For in-person sessions, your tutor comes to you. Both sides receive reminders at 24 hours and 1 hour before each session.

5

Review your child's progress report

After every session, your tutor submits a detailed progress report covering what was taught, your child's understanding level, homework assigned, and the goal for the next session. You review this in your parent dashboard.

6

Confirm, rate, and rebook

Confirm the session was delivered, leave an honest rating and review, and rebook if you are happy.

Step by Step

Your journey from application to earnings

1

Submit your application

Complete our structured tutor application with your personal details, qualifications, subjects you teach, learner levels you cover, your service area, and your preferred rate. Upload your relevant certificates for review.

📋 Applications are reviewed within 48 hours.
2

Get verified and approved

Our team reviews your application, verifies your identity, and checks your qualifications. Once approved, your profile goes live in our marketplace.

3

Set your availability

Open your availability calendar to show when you are available to teach. You are always in control of your schedule.

Families browse your profile and book sessions directly. You receive a booking notification with all the session details. Payment is already secured before the session happens.

Get paid

Once the parent confirms the session was delivered, your payment is released to your registered bank account. Build your ratings and grow your profile over time.

Safety Questions

Common questions about safety

What happens if I report a concern about a tutor? ⌄
If you report a welfare concern about any tutor, that tutor's account is immediately suspended from taking new bookings while our team investigates. We begin our review within four business hours. If the concern is upheld, the tutor is permanently removed from the platform.
Can a tutor contact my child directly outside of Mentora? ⌄
No. Every tutor signs a formal agreement that prohibits them from soliciting or maintaining contact outside the Mentora platform. Our messaging system is also designed to detect and flag any attempts to share personal contact information.
How do you check a tutor's qualifications? ⌄
Every tutor is required to upload their qualification documents during their application. Our team reviews these documents before any profile is approved. If we cannot verify a qualification, we either request additional documentation or decline the application.
What if I am not happy with a tutor's performance? ⌄
You can raise a concern within 48 hours of any session. Our support team will review the session record, the progress report, and any relevant communications. Depending on what is found, we may offer a full refund, a partial refund, a service credit, or a free replacement tutor.

The Name

Where the name Mentora comes from

Two thousand years of meaning, applied to one of Nigeria's most urgent problems.

The Root

From Homer's Odyssey. When Odysseus left for the Trojan War, he entrusted his son to a wise guardian named Mentor, the original trusted guide invested in a child's growth.
The Suffix
+ A
Latin origin. Transforms a person into a place: a destination, a platform, a movement. The 'a' signals that mentoring is not a one-off event but an ongoing relationship.

Our Story

We started with a problem every Nigerian parent knows

You have a child who is struggling. You ask around in a WhatsApp group. Someone recommends someone. Money changes hands. And then, sometimes, the tutor never shows up. The results never come. And there is no one to call.

This is not a story about bad tutors. Most tutors in Nigeria are dedicated, qualified people who love what they do. This is a story about a broken system, one with no verification, no accountability, no payment protection, no progress visibility, and no real recourse when things go wrong.

Mentora was built to fix that system. Not to replace the relationship between a tutor and a learner, but to protect it. To organise it. To make it visible, accountable, and safe for every family involved.

🛡️

What we set out to build

A fully managed platform where every tutor is verified, every payment is protected, and every session produces a real progress report. Not a directory, but a managed environment with accountability built in from the start.

👶

Safety as a first principle

Every tutor signs our child safeguarding commitment before their profile goes live. Any welfare concern triggers an immediate suspension and a same-day review. Because every child on this platform is someone's whole world.

📊

Progress that is visible

After every 2-hour session, your tutor submits a written progress report. Topics covered, understanding level, homework set, next steps. The learning is no longer a black box.

🔄

Recourse when things go wrong

If a tutor doesn't show up, we find a replacement at no extra cost or refund you in full. Your money never transfers to a tutor who did not deliver.
